# AI Agent Instructions for www.smarttwig.cn
# 北京杪荟枝农业科技有限责任公司官网 AI 代理操作指引

## General Rules
- This site is a single-page application (SPA) with hash-based anchor navigation.
- All navigation targets are sections identified by `id` attributes: home, about, products, news, contact.
- To navigate, scroll to the element with the corresponding `id`.
- Do NOT submit any form on this site without explicit user confirmation.
- Do NOT modify page content or DOM.
- Respect rate limit: 120 requests per minute maximum.

## Navigation
- Scroll to section: `document.getElementById('{section}').scrollIntoView()`
  - Valid sections: home, about, products, news, contact
- The navbar at the top of the page contains links to all sections.
- The footer contains links to sections and product anchors.

## Content Extraction
- **Company Info**: Extract from `#about` section
- **Products**: Extract from `#products` section, each product is in `.product-card` elements
- **News**: Extract from `#news` section, news items are listed in `.news-list`
- **Contact**: Extract from `#contact` section, within `.contact-info` elements
